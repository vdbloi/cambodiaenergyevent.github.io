NEW OPPORTUNITIES FOR RENEWABLE ENERGY AND GRID REINFORCEMENT IN CAMBODIA

November 29, 2022

Hyatt Regency Phnom Penh, Cambodia

OUR SPONSORS

As the COP 26 goals push more and more countries towards renewable energy, Cambodia finds itself at the right place at the right time for an energy transition. Developing more renewable energies and using non-fossil fuels is only part of what is driving new projects and opportunities, and there are many new projects and opportunities to maximize renewable energy into the power grid. But much work still needs to be done, such as upgrading the transmission network to be more efficient by employing advanced technology and by providing more renewable energy at affordable and stable prices.

What are the plans of Cambodia’s successful utility, Electricité du Cambodge? When are new tenders expected? Which proposals for renewable energy will be entertained by the EDC? Learn directly from EDC’s top officials what are the new milestones marking the development of renewables in Cambodia!
